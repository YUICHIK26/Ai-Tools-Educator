FAQ

Common Questions

What exactly is AI Tools Educator?▾
A task-oriented chatbot that helps you find the right AI tool for any job. Describe your task, get a curated list with pricing and a video tutorial for each.
Is it really free to use?▾
Yes — completely free. This is a final year academic project. We don't charge anything. We help you compare costs of other AI tools.
How many AI tools are in the database?▾
200+ AI tools across 18+ categories including image generation, writing, coding, video, audio, research, and marketing. The database is continuously updated.
Where do the video tutorials come from?▾
We curate the best tutorials from trusted YouTube creators and official tool channels. Each is vetted for quality, clarity, and relevance.
Can I compare multiple tools at once?▾
Yes! The Compare page gives side-by-side breakdowns of features, pricing tiers, free plan limits, integrations, and best-fit use cases.
Is this project open source?▾
This is a final year academic project. We plan to open-source after submission. Contact our team if you want early access or to collaborate.
